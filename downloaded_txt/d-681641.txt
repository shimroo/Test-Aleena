the largest stocking minnesota trailer dealer
absolute trailer sales
absolute trailer sales is the largest trailer dealer in the state of minnesota! we are located conveniently just south of the twin cities in inver grove heights, mn. absolute trailer sales is family owned and operated and has provided quality trailers to our customers in the twin cities, minneapolis, and st. paul metro area since 1996. we also provide trailer sales to canada and wisconsin as well as north and south dakota and iowa.

absolute trailer sales sells enclosed cargo trailers, enclosed trailers, fish houses, ice houses, utility trailers, aluminum trailers, snowmobile trailers, atv / utv trailers, equipment skidsteer and bobcat® trailers, dump trailers, concession trailers and custom trailers, car dollies, motorcycle trailers plus much, much more. we stock a complete showroom of trailer parts and accessories and we service all makes of trailers.
