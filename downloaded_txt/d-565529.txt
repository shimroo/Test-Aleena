service



established in 1992, sh lighting is a leading provider of contemporary decorative home lighting and home decor accessories. what sets us apart is our commitment to quality by manufacturing our own products which enables us to offer customization options to our customers. in the united states, our distribution warehouse serves the southern california region, providing a wide range of lamps and decor accessories for both local and nationwide pickups. we take pride in maintaining readily available stock to cater to the immediate needs of our customers.
at sh lighting, we prioritize customer satisfaction and aim to deliver exceptional value and quality in all the products we offer. our knowledgeable team is always ready to provide detailed product information and address any inquiries you may have. our mission is to create value and quality in the products that we offer.

about ussh lighting
