quality and technical innovation
fox cutlery
fox knives is the technological soul of fox group. rooted in the outdoor, sport, military and hunting knife sector for over four decades, the company has embraced input from enthusiasts worldwide with enthusiasm. engaging with a dynamic and competitive market, it has chosen to tread the path of researching and developing new ideas. with extraordinary versatility, it has implemented these ideas, managing a myriad of production variables with a "lean" approach. the company has infused them with the grit and personality of an ever-evolving design.in 1977 oreste frati, after a long experience in the production and in the commercial areas, established the fox cutlery. the professional tradition combined with the constant desire for technological innovations has built up the reputation of fox cutlery like one of the leading companies on the national and international markets.
