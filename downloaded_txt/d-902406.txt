texas barbeque



in the barn on big fossil creek











a western roots oasis, just outside the cityfort worth has changed quite a bit over the years, but at shady oak barbeque & grill we embrace the traditions that have made fort worth the gateway to the west. our slow smoked barbeque specialties and our classic texas cuisine are all served from the historic stone barn we built our restaurant around. whether eating inside the comfort of our dining room, or joining us on the grass lawn on the big fossil creek and enjoying some live music, we look forward to serving you and yours very soon.
