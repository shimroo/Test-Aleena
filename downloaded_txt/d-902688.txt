what we do
we rescue dogs from certain death at the county pounds and find them new homes.

unfortunately due to financial and time retraints we cannot accept any more dogs for rehoming.

if you have a dog that requires a new home you can contact one of the rescues on the  
ohio shelters list 
or other shelters in the tri-state area on the
river cities pets  page.
