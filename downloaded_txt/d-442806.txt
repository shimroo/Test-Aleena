committed to producing responsibly sourced jewelry and accessories with the world’s most iconic brands. 



jacmel, a family-owned company established in 1977, has one mission: to bring the world’s best and most beloved brands to consumers around the globe by partnering with the most reputable retailers. based in new york city, jacmel is a trusted leader in the jewelry and accessories industry, renowned for designing and distributing high-quality, timeless pieces crafted in collaboration with iconic brands like disney and sanrio. catering to a diverse clientele—including department stores, e-commerce platforms, and specialty retailers—jacmel strives to deliver innovative designs of the highest quality. in addition to its extensive jewelry collections, jacmel offers a range of licensed accessories, including travel essentials, jewelry organization solutions, and hair accessories. jacmel combines artistry, craftsmanship, and reliability to meet every style and lifestyle need.

































































































































proud member of the responsible jewellery council



the responsible jewellery council is an international, not-for-profit standards-setting organization that has been established to advance responsible ethical, social, and environmental practices, which respect human rights, throughout the diamond, gold, and platinum group metals jewelry supply chain, from mine to retail.
