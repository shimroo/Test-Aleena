who is cranberry shores chorus?



										 
									


cranberry shores chorus is an organization of female singers, who meet regularly to practice, perform, socialize, and have fun. we have singers of all ages, all walks of life, and from all parts of the southshore - from cape cod to wareham to the plymouth area. .
