group counseling

uhs mental health services offers several group counseling options for students looking to connect and build community with peers who have similar experiences.

 





 



badger recovery

badger recovery is uw-madison's collegiate recovery community. they offer a welcoming and judgment-free space to support you on your personal recovery journey.

 





 



cold & flu season

upper respiratory viruses are common throughout the winter months. learn how to avoid getting sick, take care of yourself when you are sick, and know when to call uhs.
