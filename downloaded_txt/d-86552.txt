about würth baer machinery
würth baer machinery is the machinery division of würth baer supply company serviced by akins machinery, hermance machine company, and j&g machinery.
each branch services a different part of the country while working together to meet the varied needs of our customers. comprised of 15+ technicians, 20+ salespeople, and 15+ support staff, the machinery division of würth baer supply company is capable of helping any customer with any project, large or small. with a combined 150,000 square feet of warehouse, the machinery division of würth baer supply company keeps new and used machinery inventory along with maintaining fully functioning showrooms. as an importer of european lines and a dealer for some of the best lines in the industry, we know what it takes to provide quality service before and after the sale. 
our goal is to be a long-term foundation of support for our customers. we look forward to earning your business!
