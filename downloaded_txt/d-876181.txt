orlando all-suite hotel
the point hotel & suites is an all suites hotel in orlando offering modern accommodations with resort amenities near orlando's most popular attractions and dining. 
explore our current hotel deals and book an upcoming stay in one of our contemporary executive studio suites, one-bedroom suites, or double king bed suites and enjoy the comfort of a home away from home. our vibrant property includes a tropical outdoor pool and hot tub, and well-equipped fitness center.
be our guest and experience the best suites in orlando at the point hotel & suites. located on universal boulevard, close to orlando's famous theme parks.
