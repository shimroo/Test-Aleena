welcome to superior trailer sales
superior trailer sales is the most comprehensive semi-trailer dealer in the south, and one of the largest independent dealers in the us.

from locations across texas and oklahoma, we stock and sell more trailers than any other dealer in the area. we are the #1 dealer in the us for fontaine and dorsey, and are regularly one of the top 3 dealers in the us for doonan and east. as a proud partner with w&b service company, the nation's largest full-service hyundai translead dealer, we offer a broad selection of new and used trailers and refrigeration units, a huge selection of trailer parts, and we can handle all of your trailer service needs.

search our inventory, give us a call, send us a note or drop by one of our locations, but most importantly,

do not buy a trailer without talking to superior first!
