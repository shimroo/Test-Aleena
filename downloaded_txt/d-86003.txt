the moment of inspiration


“i remember my epiphany that would become the quarterback academy. a drive to mentor, teach and develop new generations of quarterbacks to do better, decide faster, and reach higher. a mission to instill that special balance of mind, heart, spirit, confidence and leadership to step up when their moment comes.
now over 34 years later we feel very blessed to offer athletes a one of kind training experience.”
darin slack, qba founder








professional instruction within limited enrollment small groups


no long lines. personal interaction with our nationally-recognized staff of coaches drives improved player skill and self-confidence.








combination class room and on-field instruction


featured classroom clinics complement dynamic fieldwork sessions to dramatically improve football acumen and skill-specific techniques and movements.








fully comprehensive evaluation and testing


athletes will be tested both physically and cognitively within a full battery of assessments. athletes learn their strengths and weaknesses so they can receive optimized training.








the duel is the premier quarterback competition for rising stars!


quarterbacks come from all over the us to showcase their skills while battling other elite athletes in this 2 day national competition event.
