        about us      

the city of negaunee is a safe, friendly community with award-winning schools, first-rate medical clinics, unique shopping and dining experiences and abundant entertainment and recreational opportunities. we invite you to explore our vibrant community! please call city hall at (906) 475-7700 with any questions.
