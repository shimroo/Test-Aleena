thursday, june 07, 2012



4th of july parade grand marshals announced






the city
of elgin's 4th of july parade - presented by sherman health and the 4th of july
parade committee announced today its 2012 parade grand marshals will be the
girl scouts of northern illinois.

to
celebrate their centennial anniversary, the girl scouts of northern illinois
ceo, vicki wright, will serve as the official ambassador she will ride in a
beautiful powder blue cadillac, courtesy of elgin resident richard bennett, and
will be joined by an elgin area high school student who will be receiving her
gold award - the highest award a girl scout can earn for achievement in
community service. following right behind the grand marshal vehicle will be the
girl scouts color guard and dozens of scouts of all ages.

“the 4th
of july parade committee is privileged to have these wonderful young ladies
representing elgin as our ambassadors for the 2012 parade,” said dna promotions
coordinator jason pawlowski.

girl
scouts founder, juliette gordon low, organized the first girl scout troop on
march 12, 1912 in savannah, georgia. since then, for 100 years, girl scouts has
been building girls of courage, confidence, and character, who make the world a
better place. today, there are 3.2 million girl scouts. more than 59 million
american women enjoyed girl scouting during their childhood and that number
continues to grow as girl scouts continues to inspire, challenge, and empower
girls everywhere.

the 2012
city of elgin 4th of july parade – presented by sherman health steps off
wednesday, july 4 at 9am at slade and douglas avenues and ending at highland
and douglas avenues. the theme of this year’s parade is “red, white and food” –
a celebration of elgin’s great tastes and unique flavors.

nearly 100
parade units are expected to participate in this summertime tradition, which
typically draws more than 15,000 spectators annually to downtown elgin.

the 2012
city of elgin 4th of july parade is presented by sherman health. additional
parade sponsors include alexian brothers health system, armida & joe
dominguez, bmo harris bank, danny's pizza, elgin community college, lamp
incorporated, payday loan store, provena saint joseph hospital, psychosis
haunted house, spring hill gymnastics, waste management and woody buick.

tuesday, june 05, 2012



downtown elgin harvest market kicks off 12th season





the downtown neighborhood association is excited to kick off its 12th season of the downtown elgin harvest market – presented by alexian brothers health system this thursday, june 7 with brand new market hours from 4-8pm. located prominently in downtown elgin at 200 n. grove avenue, our weekly outdoor market will run for eighteen consecutive weeks every thursday thru october 4.

opening day at the downtown elgin harvest market will consist of:


• free alexian brothers and davey tree tote bags for customers
• more than 20 vendors with fresh vegetables, breads, olives, spices, honey, fresh cut flowers, t-shirts, artwork and concessions
• new! weekly kids row run by community partners. kids can partake in fun, on-site activities and also have a chance to win prizes by trying a new vegetable each week and showing a recently checked out gail borden public library book to one our kid’s row harvest market volunteers.  this promotion is held in conjunction with the gail borden public library’s summer reading program “reading is so delicious”.

• new! weekly cooking demonstrations with chef quincy owens.  each week chef quincy, a personal chef and elgin community college graduate, will show market patrons how to take available products at the market and transform them into a great meal or snack for your family.
• weekly live entertainment with dj greg from fun time music spinning music.
• the elgin fire department will also be on hand weekly with their trucks and various fire fighting and rescue equipment.  this is the public’s opportunity to meet and mingle with their local firefighters and become familiar with the tools of their trade.
• interactive, informational community and sponsor booths focusing on wellness, environment, sustainability, and the community.

thanks to a grant from the fit for kids program, customers will be able to use their ebt/link cards as well as credit cards at the market information booth to purchase tokens for buying products at the market.

for more details about weekly events, live entertainment and attractions at the 2012 downtown elgin harvest market, visit www.elginharvestmarket.com 

the downtown elgin harvest market is presented by alexian brothers health system and is sponsored by davey tree, waste management, armida & joe dominguez, chiro one, salon couture and simple balance holistic center. the city of elgin is a downtown elgin harvest market partner.

for more information on the downtown elgin harvest market or how to get involved, please contact harvest market manager jennifer benson at 847-488-1456 or via email at elginharvestmarket@gmail.com.

friday, april 06, 2012



2012 downtown madness champion crowned






after four intense rounds of voting that saw a record-setting 6,000 votes cast for 16 sweet elgin eateries, the public has spoken and the downtown neighborhood association of elgin is proud to announce that the winner of downtown madness 2012 is danny's pizza (302-d s. mclean boulevard) sausage and pepperoni pizza.

a trophy presentation was held thursday, april 4 at elgin public house, the downtown elgin representative in the downtown vs. crosstown final round, with representatives from both establishments present. with over 1,800 votes cast in the championship round of voting in the dna's award-winning, month-long, social media-driven restaurant promotion, danny's pizza narrowly defeated elgin public house by 96 votes.

the dna would like to offer sincere thanks to our 16 downtown madness 2012 participants and recognize their signature dishes:

the final round of downtown madness, the downtown neighborhood association of elgin's award-winning, month-long, social media-driven restaurant promotion, is underway.

it's the final countdown and after another record-breaking round of voting, madness voters have whittled the field to two terrific downtown madness finalists:

elgin public house (219 e. chicago street) turkey avocado blt versus danny's pizza (302-d s. mclean boulevard) sausage and pepperoni pizza.

both establishments will square off in the championship round of voting taking place now thru monday, april 2 at 11:59pm.

we invite you to cast your vote for your favorite dish at http://www.downtownelgin.com. only one vote per person, per valid e-mail address, per round. duplicate or incomplete ballots will not be counted.

you can also support your favorite downtown madness "teams" by encouraging friends, family members, co-workers and colleagues to get out and rock the vote for your faves! copy, paste and share the following round 4 "short form" voting link: http://conta.cc/hxsa5y. the more voters on your team's bench, the better their shot of taking the title!
