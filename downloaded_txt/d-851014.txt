welcome to new england natural sponge      
wholesale only- contact 516-678-5600 for information.
new england natural sponge co. - personal care & industrial productsconverter of custom fabricated cellulose sponge, polyurethane sponge & natural sponge products + spa, beauty care brushes, skincare accessories specializing in industrial oem applications. we also are custom fabricator of sponges for industrial and personal care products including the cosmetics & spa industry, hotel amenity and industrial manufacturing industry, ceramics, & arts and crafts.
our privately held company created in 1975 by sandy stevens, as a marketer & brand developer was an offshoot of a 4th generation family business, established in 1885, specializing in the sponge & chamois business. services include but are not limited to private label products, new england natural brands, healthcare & manufacturing.
our clients include multinational skincare marketers, beauty supply companies, the military, oil & gas pipeline companies, janitorial, and sanitary industries.new england natural sponge is also a master distributor for ettore window cleaning products and also sells leather and synthetic chamois wiping cloths to many industries.
