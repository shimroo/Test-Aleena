bodyvox presentscinevox: dance film festival



february 28 - march 2, 2025bodyvox dance center1201 nw 17th ave. portland, oregon



cinevox dance film festival is hosted annually by bodyvox in portland, oregon, usa.cinevox is dedicated to sharing innovative new dance films from around the world with portland's ever-expanding dance audience. now in its 8th year, cinevox has firmly established itself as an important venue for films of global significance.long-form or short, aggressive or whimsical, from established or emerging artists... cinevox is an open and inclusive forum designed to advance the art form of dance on film, and thereby the art of dance itself.join us for three nights of our annual cinevox dance film festival!
