   we help drive customer’s brand preference and ultimately, sales. from concept to distribution logistics, our capabilities are not limited by medium, retail channel, customer size or delivery requirements.we are committed to transforming great ideas into even greater examples of the finest quality merchandising equipment, while maximizing the visual impact of our customer products.we domestically produce and sell a variety of plastic, wood, metal/wire, acrylic and corrugate products.pfi was originally founded in 1956 by paul flum as “paul flum ideas”. over time, our name evolved to presence from innovation, to pfi instore but we still honor our founder by maintaining our original acronym, pfi.
