announce the acquisition of the songpeddler.com domain. this strategic acquisition is set to significantly enhance and extend the music category on artisanmarket.org, offering an even broader range of music-related products and services to our customers.songpeddler.com has been a respected name in online music sales, known for its dedication to promoting independent musicians and unique musical finds. by integrating songpeddler.com into the artisanmarket.org family, we are poised to offer our customers exclusive access to a more diverse array of music products, from rare instruments to original recordings and customized musical services.“our mission at artisanmarket.org has always been to connect talented artisans with consumers who appreciate the unique and handmade,” said ceo of artisanmarket.org. “with the acquisition of songpeddler.com, we are thrilled to expand our music category, reinforcing our commitment to supporting independent artists and providing our customers with an unmatched selection of distinctive music products.”the expanded music category will feature a curated collection of handcrafted musical instruments, vinyl records, bespoke songwriting services, and much more. this initiative not only broadens our product offerings but also strengthens our community of artists and musicians, providing them with a larger platform to showcase their
