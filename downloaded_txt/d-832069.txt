we serve the displaced children of georgia. did you know that the average number of displaced children in georgia primarily counts just those in foster care and does not include the hundreds of thousands of children being raised by grandparents, relatives, or fictive kin? we aim to support displaced children across the entire state by providing vital, basic needs, but we cannot achieve our goals alone. find out how you can help.
