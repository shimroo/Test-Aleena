ccd (charge-coupled device) cameras
the ikon ccd camera series for life and physical science research applications offer unique thermoelectric cooling to -100°c for market-leading low noise performance paired with highly efficient back-illuminated photon collection across a broad spectral range, and outstanding dynamic range.
features include: 

large field of view
up to 16.8 megapixel
market-leading thermoelectric cooling to -100 °c
back-illuminated > 95% qe
ultravac™ low maintenance operation







ccd for life science

applications include plant imaging, in vivo bioluminescence & bacterial luminescence.
