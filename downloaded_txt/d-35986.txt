welcome







collectiondx featured toy reviews, news, and articles from 2000 to 2024, when it finally closed. we had a good run. if you are looking for our old reviews, check out a backup of the site on the internet archive here.
we’re slowly looking into posting some of the old content on this site, but as with all things, it will take time.
in the meantime, we are still active on instagram, facebook and youtube.
if you are interested in helping out, contact us. especially if you have any experience migrating drupal content to wordpress.
