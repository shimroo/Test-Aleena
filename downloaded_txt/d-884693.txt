 tale of the cod offers a vast selection of gifts, furniture and home accessories.  nestled on main street, chatham, the tale of the cod exudes sophistication and charm. the entrance, framed by large windows, invites passersby to explore its meticulously curated selection of unique items. some of the treasures you will find at tale of the cod include:mariposa, simon pearce, swig, soundview millworks, scout bags, dash & albert, authentic models, byers’ choice and much more!  we also carry jewelry, fashion accessories, ornaments, lotions, table linens and pottery. we are proud to offer numerous items handcrafted by local artisans.
