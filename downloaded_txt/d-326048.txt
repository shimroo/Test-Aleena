prices

for many years ark-la-tex households have known the truth. for a home furniture store
featuring amazing designs and affordable prices for top brand
selections from individual pieces to whole collections for each room,
ivan smith furniture is the thinking person’s choice. prepare to be
amazed at how reasonable everything is here. 
brilliant
living room furniture ideas
 when you need an upgrade to your living room furniture
that’s chic and affordably priced, we have an array of essentials that
will both impress and make people feel at home. our selection of living room sofa sets
are in everything from victorian wood and leather to contemporary
flared looks dressed in nuvella performance fabric for family use. coffee table sets
likewise are eclectic, including classic baroque looks with curved legs
sporting craftsman details to stark minimalist tables in metal and
tempered glass. and when you really need to relax and forget about
everything, just open up one of our living room furniture
list’s top sellers of all time, a power
recliner! name a style, from french provincial to danish
modern, and either it’s on the floor or we’ll order.
serene
bedroom furniture made accessible
 set up your bedroom furniture
just right for you and that special someone with our awesome selection.
among queen beds and queen bed frames
alone, you can pick from rugged panel
beds in rustic finishes to delicate but sturdy queen anne
looks in cherry. beds in all sizes come with boldly modern statements
such as upholstered headboards and even shelving and usb ports for
bedtime readers and checking your social media. from trim bedroom nightstands
to handy chests of
drawers, it’s all here for less.

delectable
dining room furniture for your gatherings
 our home
furniture store makes the art of entertaining seem
virtually effortless. when it comes time to entertain the family for
the holidays, never panic again thanks to our dining room furniture
choices from simple 3-piece
counter-height dinettes to grand traditional extension tables as
part of classic 7-piece
dining room sets.

impossible dream. when you shop ivan smith’s ark-la-tex home furniture stores
