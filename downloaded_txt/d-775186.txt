bearing housings have been around since the bronze age and have kept industries moving for thousands of years. over time, these products have evolved to become better suited to their purpose. as one of the leading bearing housing designers, manufacturers and suppliers, arvis is at the centre of this evolution. well-known for high-quality products and excellent service, we design to your requirements.
