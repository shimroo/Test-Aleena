soft-sel®, salt for water softening

do not pick just any salt for water softening. after all, the quality level of your water largely depends on the salt you use. soft-sel® is salt of food grade quality. giving you soft and pure water without a care at all times. the high purity level of soft-sel® makes sure your water softener works better and enjoys a longer service life.
