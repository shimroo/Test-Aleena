helping utilities keep the lights on since 1991.



utility system reliability starts at the core.your commitment to system reliability has customers spoiled: they expect the lights to come on every time they flip the switch. our economy – and way of life – depends on it.you’ve invested millions in distribution and transmission system technology to provide your customers superior service. but at the end of the day, your electric grid is only as reliable as the weakest utility pole in your network.weather, decay and insects – these are the enemies that literally attack the core of your system. at intec, we’ve got the solution.since 1991, intec’s preservation and testing services have helped to keep the lights on for hundreds of utilities across the country and the globe. our in-service pole inspection techniques have raised the bar for accuracy and cost effectiveness.
