statement regarding 'flash' player and soil-net
  adobe flash was discontinued on december 31st 2020. soil-net depended heavily on flash and unfortunately has been severely affected by this. it is our intention to re-work soil-net in the future using more contemporary tools, so please be patient while this is underway. meanwhile we will seek to patch soil-net so it remains usable. thanks for your continued support for the project.
