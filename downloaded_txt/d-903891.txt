hotels near volcanoes national park

looking for affordable hotels near volcanoes national park hawaii? kilauea hospitality offers a variety of surprisingly inexpensive lodging options. we’re nestled in an amazing spot – a true tropical rainforest. it’s absolutely beautiful. we’re near the town of volcano village, and only 3 miles from the spectacular volcanoes national park. our rainforest location is perfect for exploring the big island.
chalet kilauea hotel is the choice for travelers seeking rooms individually designed and crafted with great attention to detail. lokahi lodge is moderate and comfortable. volcano hale provides basic accommodations for travelers on a budget. the three properties are just a short drive from each other.
the big island of hawaii is much larger than other hawaiian islands. a day is really not enough – everyone wishes for more. staying for several days allows you to see volcanoes national park in a way you just can’t do in one day. there are multiple must-see drives and great hikes you won’t want to miss.
see our hawaii travel blog for our favorite not-to-miss suggestions for exploring the big island.
