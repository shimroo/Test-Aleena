the seattle home & garden show, presented by skydadu, is the northwest’s largest consumer home show with everything for the home, indoors and out – an annual show in downtown seattle at lumen field event center. the spring show present hundreds of exhibits including builders, remodelers, farm to table feature, decorating, landscaping…everything from floor to ceiling and beyond. attendees also enjoy special features including seminars and special appearances by dr lori.
