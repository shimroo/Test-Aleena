welcome friends & neighbors across allegan & ottawa county! 



you've been redirected here because united way of ottawa and allegan counties has joined forces with heart of west michigan united way. together, we are now one regional nonprofit organization committed to serving allegan, kent, and ottawa counties.for over a century, our united ways have been dedicated to reducing poverty and empowering communities across west michigan. by unifying, we can combine our resources and strengths to address our region's most pressing needs more effectively and make a greater impact.














what this means for youas a supporter of united way of ottawa and allegan counties, you are now part of a single organization more equipped and powered to meet the evolving needs of our west michigan region. heart of west michigan united way will continue to uphold the same values and dedication you have always supported, now with an expanded reach and enhanced capabilities.
