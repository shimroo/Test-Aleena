excellence in education
usf's dept. of chemical, biological and materials engineering is a leader in innovating
                  best practices in undergraduate and graduate education. ongoing undergraduate initiatives
                  include implementation of a peer leader program, development of a pedagogy for teaching
                  differential equations in engineering context, and roll-out of take-home kits for
                  hands on engineering learning.
