ugly johns custom boats
family owned and operated since 1974
discover the premier destination for all your boating needs at ugly john's. with dealership and marina locations spanning grand lake, beaver lake, lake eufaula, and tulsa, our extensive presence throughout oklahoma and arkansas ensures convenience and quality service for enthusiasts across the region.

as your trusted dealer, we offer an impressive selection of new and used custom boats from renowned brands such as nautique, monterey, premier pontoons, sea ray, and pardo. whether you're in search of bowriders, cruisers, yachts, ski boats, wakeboard boats, or more, our diverse inventory caters to every preference and lifestyle.

beyond our exceptional boat offerings, we provide comprehensive parts and service solutions to keep your vessel operating at peak performance. our team of experts is dedicated to ensuring your boating experience is nothing short of exceptional, with prompt and reliable assistance whenever you need it.

for a delightful break from the water, visit our full-service bar and grille at thunder bay marina on grand lake. relax and unwind in a picturesque setting while enjoying amazing food and refreshing beverages.

at ugly john's, we're more than just boats – we're your ultimate destination for unparalleled boating experiences and exceptional service. explore our offerings today and elevate your time on the water with us.
