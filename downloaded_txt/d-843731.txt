                    home                


solution - dust free























 









discover the benefits of filtration and purification
welcome to a world where clean air is no longer a luxury but a necessity. our range of dust free indoor air quality (iaq) products is designed to cater to you and your family's needs. bid farewell to airborne pollutants and say hello to a healthier home environment. our cutting-edge solutions allow you to breathe easily, knowing your indoor air is free from dust and other harmful particles.





dust free’s iaq3 strategy integrates three levels of air quality products, each increasing the overall air quality of the space. the result is a dynamic air quality management strategy that provides a comprehensive iaq solution, custom-tailored to speciﬁc needs.








filtration
permanent and disposable air filtration removes dust, pollen, mold, and dander as air circulates through your central air system.




purification
air purification using multiple next-generation technologies can reduce odors and vocs while inactivating bacteria and viruses.




uv inactivation
germicidal uv lights work 24/7 to inactivate biological contaminants at the hvac system level where the uv light shines. even when your hvac system isn’t actively running.
