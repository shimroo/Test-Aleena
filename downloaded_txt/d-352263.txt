            traffic counting with metrocount
      

for over three decades, we've been at the forefront of traffic monitoring technology. our low-power and highly accurate traffic counters deliver data that helps create more efficient and safer roads, cycleways, and shared paths worldwide. with a presence in 135 countries, we combine precision engineering with dedicated support to empower your data-driven decisions.







check_circle


            tech that lasts
      

with a life expectancy of 10+ years, our counters are fully weatherproof and continue operating through extreme temperatures, floods and fires.

  what data do you need?
  
consider the goal of your study. are you looking to get more information on cars, cyclists or pedestrians? 







        02
      


  what traffic data timeframe do you need?
  
are you considering a 1-2 week study, or do you need to collect data long-term and potentially ongoingly?







        03
      


  do you require real-time data?
  
the roadpod vm offers instant access to live road traffic data, including but not limited to volumes, speeds, classification, etc. 







        04
      


  how will you collect your traffic data?
  
do you want to receive your data via cloud, or will you manually download your data at the survey site?







        05
      


  what are your traffic data analysis goals?
  
choose between the comprehensive mte software or the user-friendly atlyst® dashboard. mte has a learning curve but unlimited potential, while atlyst offers immediate traffic insights.
