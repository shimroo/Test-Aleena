webmaster
jonathan yardley has been covering high school ice
hockey in new jersey since 1996. a native of morristown and a delbarton
alumnus, he graduated from rice university in houston, texas in 2005. a radio
and television broadcaster, he called games for the 2003 ncaa championship rice
baseball team (2002-05) and the 2011 major league soccer eastern conference
champion houston dynamo (2008-11). he currently works as a free-lance
broadcaster for espn, msg, nextvr, and others.
 
he grew up watching hockey at mennen arena and for
five seasons (1996-97 to 2000-01) wrote a weekly newsletter and ran the webpage
for the mcssihl, the morris county high school league. he wrote for hockey
night in boston through the completion of the 2015-16 season.
 
this site is a
resource for high school hockey fans from the whole state and is the most
accurate source for schedules, standings, and scores in new jersey.
