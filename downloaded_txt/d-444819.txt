welcome to accent homes


 for more than 40 years accent homes, inc has worked to become one of northwest indiana's leading home builders by
        providing high quality, affordable entry level and move-up homes for buyers in lake, porter, jasper, newton and
        laporte counties in indiana. we have active communities where we build new homes in merrillville, hobart, crown
        point, cedar lake, lowell, demotte, valparaiso, portage. we also build in schererville, dyer, highland, st john,
        griffith and many other northwest indiana communities.
    

customizable plans at all price points!
        we offer more than 40 customizable plans that can be built almost anywhere in northwest indiana. choose colors
        and options. make reasonable floor plan changes. our plans are all computerized.
    

our lot-your lot-spot lot
        we offer many communities where we have inventory lots or we can build on your lot or on "spot lots." you pick
        out the lot and we will buy the lot and make it part of a house/lot package for you (limitations and conditions
        apply). call or come out today to get answers to all your questions.
    
contact us today for an appointment to meet with a new home consultant.
