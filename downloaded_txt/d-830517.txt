data center cooling pumps





























































liquid cooling pumps and intelligent controls

moog’s coremotion™ magnetic pump with matching controller is designed for pumping coolant in direct-to-chip and immersion liquid cooling applications for heat removal. by integrating the rotor magnetics and the impeller into a single piece, our designs minimize the pump size and eliminate rotating seals, resulting in more compact pumps with fewer leak points. moog’s coremotion™ pumps are paving the way for data center cooling solutions.
