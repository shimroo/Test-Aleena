the comfort technology company®
a fortune 500® company headquartered in southern california, skechers has spent over 30 years helping men, women and kids everywhere look and feel good. developing comfort technologies is at the foundation of all that we do—delivering stylish, innovative, and quality products at a reasonable price. from our diverse footwear offering to a growing range of apparel and accessories, skechers is a complete lifestyle brand.
with international business representing over half of our total sales, we have product available in approximately 180 countries and significant opportunities for continued expansion worldwide. we sell our collections direct to consumers through approximately 5,300 skechers stores around the globe and skechers e-commerce sites, as well as through a network of third-party partners.
a multibillion-dollar global leader in the footwear industry.
