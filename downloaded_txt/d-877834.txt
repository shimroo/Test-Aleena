the bike hubknowledgeable. passionate.  experienced. serving de pere and greater green bay since 2001.









welcome to the hubhere at the bike hub, we are passionate about outdoor recreation, from cycling in the warmer months to cross country skiing, snowshoeing, and fat biking when the snow starts to fly. the friendly and knowledgeable team at the hub is here to help you with your next ride, ski, or whatever adventure is waiting for you.








e-bikesare you looking to keep up with your riding partner? how about saving some gas money and using a bike for your commute? an e-bike may be what you're looking for!brands include: giant, liv, momentum, specialized, and addmotorlearn more


fat bikesa wonderful option for winter fitness, fat biking is very popular around this region. these bikes are great to pedal in snow, and fun on the single track trails to keep you active all year.brands include: corvus and giantlearn more


mountain bikeswe are lucky here in ne wisconsin with many great mountain bike trails within a short drive. with all sorts of different types of trails, we have the right bike that will fit your riding needs!brands include: transition, giant, liv, specializedlearn more








road bikeswith gorgeous country roads being plentiful in our wonderful state, having the proper bike that will carry you for miles is a must. check out our selection of road and gravel bikes!brands include: colnago, time, giant, liv, specializedlearn more


comfort and hybrid bikesa great ride for those that want to put in some miles with a lightweight bike without compromising comfort, hybrids are versatile bikes that are great for paved or packed surfaces. if you are looking for a nice upright bike to cruise around town, a comfort bike would be great for you!brands include: giant, liv, specialized,  and jamislearn more


recumbent bikesrecumbent bikes are built for all ages and abilities. they are also a great option for those that may not be able to ride a traditional two wheeled bike.brands include: catrike, terratrikelearn more








cross country skis and snowshoeswe are northeast wisconsin's source for cross country skis and snowshoes!cross country skiing and snowshoeing are a great way to stay active during our winter months here in wisconsin. these activities allow for you to get out and enjoy the peaceful nature surrounding you, all while getting your heart rate up and building some muscle. stop in today and we can get you set up with all of the equipment needed! click here to learn more!we are proud to announce that we have a skate ski group, gnarski! click here to learn what we offer!brands include: fischer, salomon, atomic, leki, tubbs
