ywca metropolitan chicago is dedicated to eliminating racism, empowering women and promoting peace, justice, freedom and dignity for all.














what we do ↘regardless of race or economic status, the ywca metropolitan chicago provides individuals and families with the support and tools they need to heal from trauma, obtain skills and credentials, access resources and opportunities, and build stronger communities. the ywca metropolitan chicago is dedicated to promoting racial justice and personal empowerment across all of our programs and services.



































driving economic equity

we offer people, particularly women of color, with job skills training, financial sustainability, and asset acquisition to help close the racial wealth gap.




learn more











unleashing youth & family potential

we connect families to childcare resources and build the capacity of childcare providers, while empowering the next generation of youth through engaging afterschool programs.




learn more











advancing healing, safety & belonging

we provide comprehensive counseling services to individuals impacted by all forms of violence, including gender-based and community-based violence, and also promote community wellness and equal access to healthcare resources.




learn more


















reach out ↘ywca metropolitan chicago provides direct services, empowerment programming, and advocacy to support marginalized individuals and families, especially women and girls of color, while working to challenge structural barriers to equity.
