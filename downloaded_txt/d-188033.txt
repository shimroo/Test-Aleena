spring primary election 


the next election is the spring primary, scheduled for february 18, 2025.  this election will feature just one contest on the ballot:  wisconsin superintendent of public instruction.						read on...







get your taxes done for free at new berlin public library 


tax assistance is available at the new berlin public library on wednesdays, february 5-april 9, 2025.  if you are interested in getting your taxes done for free - please see the instructions or call the new berlin public library at 262-754-1830.			additional info...







miss wisconsin crown camp 2025 


come out to the new berlin activity and recreation center on saturday, april 26th and participate in the miss wisconsin crown camp 2025!  this is for girls ages 5-12.			additional info...







new berlin arc's one year celebration 


come out and celebrate the new berlin arc's 1 year celebration!			additional info...







arc will be closed for spring election 


the new berlin recreation center will be closed in observance of the spring primary election starting on february 17th at 10:00am and all day on february 18th.  normal business hours will resume on february 19th.						read on...
