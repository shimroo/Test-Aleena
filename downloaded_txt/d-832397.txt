why 
          baldwin hardware for my home?

          is the premier supplier of solid brass door hardware in the nation we are proud 
          to be selling their complete lines on door hardware, bath hardware, cabinet hardware, 
          exterior lighting, and interior decor.
forged 
          brass not cast, this allows the finish on to be blemish free and the hardware 
          to shine. cast products will often have blemishes where air was trapped in the 
          casting.

          available are passage sets, door stops, mail slots, door bells and exterior lighting. 
          no other manufacturer offers as many items.

          interior of the passage and privacy locks. lesser competitors have exposed screws
