full-service landscape design, installation & maintenance



since 1977, new garden landscaping & nursery has been the triad's foremost resource for landscape and horticultural services. we are dedicated to providing value and building long-term relationships with our employees, customers and communities. new garden landscaping & nursery proudly serves the triad, working primarily in and around greensboro, high point and winston-salem. we take great pride in our experienced landscape designers and our skilled landscape installation, landscape maintenance, and retail customer service teams.
