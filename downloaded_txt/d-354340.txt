wheels in motion - santa barbara









wheels in motion of santa barbara is a respected dealership in the motorsports industry that has been in the santa barbara/goleta community for over 55 years. we are a trusted, family-owned business offering a wide selection of stock and delivering exemplary customer service to all of our customers. we are operated by experienced sales, parts, and service employees, some of whom have been with us for over 10 years. our award-winning service department mechanics are factory certified. we are proud to continue this tradition in serving the central and south coast from our larger goleta location on 6466 hollister ave. we know how hard you work for the time to relax and enjoy your family and friends. our mission is simple. our commitment to you, our customer, is to make sure your vehicle operates problem-free. we take pride in our personal relationship with you, our customer, and focus on meeting and striving to exceed your expectations.
