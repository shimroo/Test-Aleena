accessibility statement artsplosure is committed to increasing accessibility and broadening access to the arts through our mission art for all. we believe that everyone should have the opportunity to experience the arts. everyone is welcome at artsplosure. artsplosure works to create a welcoming and safe environment for people with disabilities to experience our festivals and art.



diversity, equity & inclusion purpose statementwe believe in our mission of art for all. we believe that access to the arts has the power to create positive change in communities through the art and events we get to present to all ages and abilities. artsplosure works to remove the barriers that prejudice and privilege have created so that everyone in our community has equitable access to arts and culture.
