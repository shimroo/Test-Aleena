join us for worship



with a rich history and a deep sense of mission, we offer worship, fellowship, and outreach opportunities for all ages. through online and in-person services, meaningful programs, and a dedication to empathy and compassion, calvary seeks to make a positive impact in the annapolis area and beyond, building a community that reflects christ's love and grace.join us for our in-person or virtual service.
