welcome to the whale's tooth pub!
the whale's tooth pub, open year-round, is one of maine's most popular gastro pubs, where it's all about the food, mixology and personal service! nestled between camden and belfast, the whale’s tooth pub is located on 400 feet of beautiful oceanfront in lincolnville, voted among the top 20 most picturesque towns in the united states according to outdoor magazine. using fresh ingredients from farm and sea, we passionately serve delicious pub fare, including our famous fish 'n chips, voted top 10 in maine by best things maine! we also delight with elevated seasonal specials, fresh steamed lobsters, and tempting craft cocktails! we hope you enjoy our unique culinary twist, served inside a 1777 classic english pub with a priceless view, and a huge roaring fire during the winter! check out our about page for events, our wtp on tap newsletter and coupons! reservations are highly encouraged friday-sunday evenings, year-round. we also host private parties! scroll down to see the flavors and goings-on. we very much look forward to serving you happiness! ~ cheers! chris & martha nickerson, ownersreservations
