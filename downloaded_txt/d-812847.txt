men's, women's and kid's footwear
shoes.co.uk is the place to shop for designer footwear and accessories. we offer a great selection of shoes for men, women and children of all ages (and shoe size!) only the most original, well crafted products make it onto our site, so you are guaranteed quality, comfort and style with every purchase. from aerosoles to azor and fantasy to front  - there is bound to be something for everyone in our fabulous shoe shop. at our head office in wells, somerset, we take as much pride in our customer service as we do in our shoes. we operate a no nonsense, free returns policy on every item and free delivery on orders over £50, otherwise £3.99. shoes.co.uk is part of the pgp (holdings) group, take a look at our sister sites www.shoon.com and www.shuropody.com. seo and ecommerce support by apex ecommerce
