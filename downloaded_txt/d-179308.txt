welcome to camp swataralocated at the foot of beautiful blue mountain in northwestern berks county, pennsylvania, camp swatara provides opportunities in the natural setting for persons of all ages to grow in their relationship with jesus christ, so that they may live their lives for him in total discipleship.




    learn more
  

 













faith formed.  memories made.  god glorified.



camp swatara is excited to announce openings for year-round staff opportunities!

 


    learn more
  















summer campwe offer a variety of summer camp programs for campers of all ages. from day camp and beginners to youth musical and mountainside to monarch camps for adults with intellectual disabilities, we have a camp for you! join us at camp swatara, where faith is formed, memories are made and god is glorified.




    learn more
  















family campgroundour family-friendly campground has lots to offer!  we have two playgrounds, lots of open space for games and sports, over 5 miles of hiking trails on 750+ acres, a splash pad, shared olympic-sized swimming pool, a 4-acre lake for boating or fishing.  campers can choose to stay in rustic cabins, tent or rv sites.




    learn more
  















retreat facilitiescamp's many retreat facilities provide a natural, relaxed and prayerful atmosphere for retreats, seminars, meetings, leadership training's.  from hotel-style accommodations complete with food service to rustic cabins, camp can accommodate just about any group in whatever setting you prefer.
