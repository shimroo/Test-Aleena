the worcester cultural coalition (wcc) is a public-private partnership between the city of worcester and over 70 cultural organizations. its mission is to draw on worcester’s rich and diverse cultural assets to foster economic revitalization, support active, creative engagement for all and promote a strong cultural identity for greater worcester
