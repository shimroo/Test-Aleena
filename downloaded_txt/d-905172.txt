providing safe and dependable horse transportation since 1978















judge manning horse transportation, inc. was founded by mr. judge manning in 1978 with just one truck. the company expanded over the years, growing to its present size by operating a fleet of clean, modern, and meticulously maintained equipment. our drivers are safe and experienced professionals, providing exceptional and dependable service to our customers. 















judge manning horse transportation is a leader in the horse transportation industry, exceeding all us department of transportation standards and regulations. the company has been a proud member of the national horse carriers association since 1983, with judge himself having been the long-time treasurer of the organization.
