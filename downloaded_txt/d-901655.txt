who we are



the michigan gateway community foundation is a multifaceted, philanthropic organization leading the way to positive change.we initiate, protect, nurture and create philanthropic efforts focused on everlasting positive impact in our local communities within south berrien and cass counties.



an impact you can see.our grantees are changing lives throughout our service area. from education to health & wellness and culture, mgcf is dedicated to supporting and enhancing the quality of life in our local communities.
