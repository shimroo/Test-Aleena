what the nlna does for you:



maintains liberty lands, a community owned greenspace serving the neighborhood with a playground, picnic area, community garden, composting, and events.provides a voice to neighbors on new developments and zoning changes through the zoning committee which makes recommendations to city agencies.hosts neighborhood events such as meet and greets, secondhand swaps, plant sales, and story slams.organizes regular town halls for residents to provide feedback and get involved in neighborhood initiatives .operates the community center, a place that’s available for neighbors to meet and discuss community issues, or to rent for private events.runs clean & green programs and initiatives that include storm water management, and street tree management.
