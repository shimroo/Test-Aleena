july 24, 2024
 

the school of integrated sciences empowers students to analyze and solve real-world, human problems by integrating scientific, technological, business, and social aspects of these problems, and to communicate innovative solutions to a diverse audience.









   

 
our students and faculty study the application of a broad range of sciences and technologies to problems of current societal importance. 
our majors emphasize hands-on learning, holistic thinking, and innovative approaches to challenging issues. 
our students and faculty partner together to create and communicate solutions to a wide range of societal challenges.
