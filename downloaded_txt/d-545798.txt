          to view these pages properly with arabic web fonts, please follow the
          instructions below: 

on your internet
              explorer (running under windows xp or later) menu bar click
              view/encoding then uncheck "auto-select" and check
              "arabic (windows)". if "arabic (windows)" is
              not available, check under "more" in the same menu and
              select "arabic (windows)" from there.
on your netscape
              navigator menu click view/character encoding, then click
              "arabic (windows 1256)". if "arabic (windows
              1256)" is not available, check under more/middle eastern and
              select "arabic (windows 1256)" from there. go to
              view/character encoding/auto-detect and choose "off".
