ppe (personal protective equipment)
27/09/2020
since the outbreak of covid-19 the enquiry of personal protective equipment has significantly increased and thru our online meeting we helped buyers all over the
world to source for mask, glove, face-shield, medical gown etc. thanks to our ppe suppliers, we have cooperated numerous meeting and become an important sourcing provider to our buyers. 





tradeeasy expanding online meeting
20/09/2020
the covid-19 pandemic has undoubtedly driven enterprise leader to rethink the way they operate their businesses, in order to keep business as usual without disruption,
buyer and supplier have turned to video conference with their clients or colleagues. the new form of work make enterprises to transform the way they work and to adopt more on video meeting.
tradeeasy has been using video conferencing since 2010 and with our experience to host online conference our meeting number has significant increased by double.
today tradeasy is serving over a hundred meetings per month and steady increasing.





tradeeasy at the parisian fashion fairyland
11/02/2019
for the very first time, tradeeasy exhibited in texworld paris at the heart of fashion.
twelve high quality fashion manufactureres were featured at tradeeasy's showcase during the 4 days show at the fashion fairyland.
significant responses were received with 150+ individual buyer leads mainly from european chainstore buyer, wholesaler, online retailer, etc.
