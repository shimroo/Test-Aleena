captrust’s phoenix-based advisors specialize in providing comprehensive wealth management services to high-net-worth individuals, business owners, and families, as well as investment advisory services to fiduciaries of corporate retirement plans, and endowments and foundations of nonprofits. the team enjoys working directly with clients in all areas of financial and investment strategy, specializing in holistic tax, retirement, and estate planning.
