 more than 50 years ago, the
tucker business association (tba) was
established by local small businesses, local corporations and independent professionals
to promote and enhance the tucker business community. since its incorporation as a non-profit organization in 1992, tba has expanded its membership to include
community organizations, government entities, and other non-profit groups.our mission is to serve as the unified voice of the men and women who form the business backbone of the tucker community.we invite you to join us in building the tucker business community and sharing the success that comes from networking with your peers and serving the community together.

    why join the tba?
 
membership in the tucker business association (tba) has many benefits. annual dues are $100 per year and your membership last for 12 months.
what are the benefits? networking, both intentional and continual that can help create customers, relationships, partnerships, employees, and more.educational/learning opportunities from local business & civic experts which help you on the cutting edge and 'in the know'opportunities to promote your business, products, services, organization or causelistings in our
online member directory that has
seo value and can help you be found and create leadsopportunties to meet local civic leaders from tucker, dekalb county and moremembers of tba receive a discount for their dekalb chamber of commerce membershipdid we mention that we are a really fun group?
join tba today to take advantage of all the benefits!﻿ 
 networking & professional 
development opportunities
 
join us for one of our events; our monthly educational luncheons; our after-hours events for b2b networking, appetizers and cocktails; or one of the other events special events that we have.  you can learn about our events by following tba on social media, joining our mailing list, or viewing our coming events calendar.
