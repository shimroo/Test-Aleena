who we are —
our mission is to improve the lives of working families—to bring economic justice to the workplace and social justice to our state and the nation. to accomplish this mission, we work to:





unite 10.5 million working women and men of every race and ethnicity and from every walk of life.





strengthen the voice of michigan working families at all levels of government and in a changing global economy.





unite 10.5 million working women and men of every race and ethnicity and from every walk of life.





strengthen the voice of michigan working families at all levels of government and in a changing global economy.





support michigan workers as they bargain with employers to improve their living conditions and workplaces, as well as their communities, state and nation.





build a broad movement of michigan workers by helping workers join and form unions.





— what we do
the michigan afl-cio is the state federation of labor, representing over 1,000,000 active and retired members of 40+ unions throughout the state.
