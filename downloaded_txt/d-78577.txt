camps baco & che-na-wah




we’re two separate camps built on a single tradition. the secret to a great camp is its people.




    learn more
  



























we are delighted to be celebrating more than 100 years of fun, thrilling adventures, lasting friendships, and cherished memories.



camp che-na-wah was established in 1923 and camp baco in 1951 as family-run camps along the shores of tranquil lake balfour in the adirondack mountains in upstate new york.














watch our welcome video trailer.



watch our welcome video trailer to get a preview of the adventures, laughter, and friendships that await at camps baco & che-na-wah. from outdoor challenges to campfire camaraderie, our camp is all about creating lifelong memories and friendships.
