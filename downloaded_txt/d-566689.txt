welcome to flatlandsupply.com
we are proud to offer the van wert area quality electrical, plumbing, voice, & data supplies, along with personal customer service
				that's hard to find at the "big box" stores.
while we used to describe flat lands as offering "everything that you don't see in a house" ... such as wire, breaker
				boxes, plumbing pipes, sump pumps, water heaters ... over the years we have expanded our product lines to include bathtubs, toilets, showers,
				faucets, lavatories, cabinets, countertops, decorative lighting, and more.
call us or stop in and browse our wide, self-serve aisles. allow our knowledgeable staff to assist you and answer your questions.


mission statement

our mission is to supply a vast selection of quality electrical, plumbing, voice, and data supplies at a fair and reasonable price to
					contractor, commercial, industrial, school, government, agricultural, and residential customers.
we aim to offer exceptional customer service to the northwest ohio counties of van wert, paulding, putnam, allen, auglaize, and
					mercer and several counties in northeast indiana, along the state line and beyond.
