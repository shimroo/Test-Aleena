triton investments is a local family owned business that primarily builds and manages apartment communities in the mountain west. with over thirty years in the industry, we have devoted our expertise to building and maintaining quality apartment homes. our apartment homes are well-designed, large and competitively priced with no shortcuts in the quality of construction or management. we consider our company much more than just a business. triton investments has been established to provide an honorable living for our employees, our families and our investors by creating caring  communities for our residents and not just developing apartment properties.
