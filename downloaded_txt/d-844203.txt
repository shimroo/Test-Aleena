educating and empowering young artists.




    book services
  














on location education is the premier educational consulting service for young performers. we offer complete coordination of on-set education, permitting, and child labor compliance services for film, television, and theatre productions in addition to an array of custom and private offerings. we provide services around the globe, including the united states, canada, the united kingdom, and europe. 


















on-set services

on location education offers an array of on-set services for child performers. from studio teachers to child labor coordinators to welfare workers, we maintain a global roster of teachers and industry professionals ready to work on your next project.

our work

throughout our forty year history, we have worked on thousands of projects around the globe. each of our production services include robust administrative support, thorough labor law expertise, and access to an expansive network of teachers and welfare specialists.
