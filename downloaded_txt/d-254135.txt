a message from our pastor




welcome to
spirit of the cross church!













we appreciate you taking a few minutes to learn more about us. we understand visiting a new church can be a stressful experience, which is why we do everything possible to make everyone feel welcome. spirit of the cross is not just a place to go to on sunday; we are family in which everyone can belong. we hope you will decide to join us for worship or attend an upcoming event. we look forward to meeting you soon!
