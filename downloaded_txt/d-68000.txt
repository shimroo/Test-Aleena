welcome to bailey's funeral and cremation carewe hope our website will offer ease of access to obituaries for people entrusted to our care, grief resources as well as information regarding funerals, final gatherings, cremation options, pre-arrangements and monuments.as a family, we are grateful to serve families during one of the most difficult times in life. you will find that our dedicated and compassionate staff have the experience and knowledge necessary to offer the most options and best value to assist you when making choices concerning your loved one. we are available to answer any questions you may have prior to, during or following the loss of your loved one.we also provide guidance and grief support which is free of charge and available at any time. follow us on facebook and feel free to contact us any time by telephone or email. being locally owned and operated we are able to make ourselves available when it’s convenient for you. our toll free number in saskatchewan is:1-888-783-7552 or dial 1-306-783-7552. you may also contact us by email: reception@baileysfuneralhome.com
