welcome to my website
photo by robin damore
i am isadora alman, mft, cst a california licensed psychotherapist and counselor and a board certified sexologist. i am a former radio talk show host and a lecturer and workshop leader on a variety of communications topics. “ask isadora,” my syndicated advice column, appeared in news weeklies nationwide for more than twenty-five years. many of the q and a’s from the columns appear on this site.
you can read my essays online on psychology today (www.psychologytoday.com/blog/sex-sociability) and check out my published books and pamphlets under books on this site.
i have retired from my private counseling practice after more than 35 years.
