    our history
  
























group lodging
for  groups visiting philadelphia, chamounix offers clean, safe and comfortable accommodations.   each bed has a 30” x 74” bio-pruf innerspring mattress.  the mansion can host up to 40 overnight guests.  the carriage house has three, 10-bed dorms and a separate room for the group’s leader.  the setting is quiet and private, minutes from philadelphia’s cultural, historic and educational destinations.
