with over forty years of experience in the bearings industry, midland bearings are the uk’s preferred trade supplier.
we are the sole uk and ireland distributor for fk bearing units and the exclusive uk stockists of mba brand ball and taper roller bearings.
with our unrivalled selection of top quality products and affordable prices, coupled with next day delivery and state of the art product cataloguing, it’s no surprise that midland bearings is one of the country’s largest importers and wholesalers of bearings.
midland bearings holds over £17 million of stock covering over 20 million components in our central west midlands base, with a huge choice of bearings from chinese, japanese and western european manufacturers. looking for something specific? browse our catalogue or contact our knowledgeable team for assistance.
