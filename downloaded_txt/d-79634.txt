 01 about astra suites
 welcome to astra suites
your personal
santorini suite paradise



as we welcome you to astra suites, you will be humbled by santorini’s beauty. with mesmerising views of the caldera and santorini’s famed sunsets, matched by our award-winning service, your stay at astra suites is a distinctly luxurious experience.
a new level of luxury at santorini’s all-suites retreat. exclusive luxury overlooking the aegean.
your personal santorini escape

 








astra suites is currently listed in the top 25 hotels of the world, as chosen by tripadvisor members.
the property is also a three times world travel awards winner for greece’s leading all suites hotel 2016,2017 and 2018.
in 2017 the hotel was named classic europe independent hotel partner of the year by classic vacations, cannes.
the hotel also won most romantic hotel for 2017 and best service hotel 2016 at the condé nast johansens annual awards, as well as gold in the category of excellence in customer service at the greek tourism awards 2015.
