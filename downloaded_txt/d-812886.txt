the wawasee list is a site for former students and teachers from wawasee high 
school to congregate and share information. the goal of this list is to 
provide a forum which allows former students to easily find information on 
fellow classmates. if you attended wawasee high school, you are invited to 
add your name to this list. if you have any questions about this list you 
can contact the maintainer. 


the wawasee list currently contains information on

1409 former
students of wawasee high school 
(located in 
syracuse, indiana). 
this list is monitored and maintained
by  


greg hormann. any
questions, comments or suggestions should be directed to maintainer@wawasee-list.net.
