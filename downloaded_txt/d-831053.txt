our mission 



business and professional people working together to make their community a better place for everyone to live and work. 



the weirton area chamber of commerce is a voluntary, non-profit partnership of business and professional people working together towards a common goal — to build a healthy economy and to improve the quality of life in our community. the business of the chamber is to enable its members to do together what they might not be able to do individually. we believe the effectiveness and success of our chamber depends on our credibility and representation of the community’s business interests.
