parties picnics & promotions
parties, picnics and promotions (ppp) provides fun, interactive experiences for all types of events throughout texas, new mexico, louisiana, and oklahoma. our vast inventory of unique and innovative items will enhance your special events, promotions, corporate events, team-building events, trade shows, conventions, holiday functions, project graduations, fundraisers, and any other events! we provide customized and creative events for any occasion and will work with you to ensure your event is a success. ppp has been a party rental and entertainment service leader for over 20 years.

integrity is what we strive for, whether the event is a 15,000 attendee corporate event or a small intimate party.  several members of our staff have been with the company since it opened its doors twenty years ago.  ppp party rental employees average over 10 years experience operating our equipment.
