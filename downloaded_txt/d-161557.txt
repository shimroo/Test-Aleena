rooms & suites 



experience our stunning, comfortable, well-equipped, oversized rooms and suites, with an option to suit everyones stay needs 



















 






executive suites 



									indulge in our 4th-floor suite: master bedroom with ensuite, living room with an extra full bathroom, and captivating pool views. book your luxury escape now! 								






















 






kreole rooms 



									located in a separate block in the grounds of coco palm, kreole rooms are closer to rodney bay village, the entertainment capital of saint lucia. 								






















 






luxe swim up 



									open the french doors to your private patio with steps leading directly to the pool in these premium ground floor rooms. 								






















 






luxury suites 



									experience breathtaking views from our 4th-floor suites overlooking the pool, rodney bay marina, and lush hills of saint lucia. 								






















 






patio rooms 



									open the french doors and step from your room on to patio set within lush tropical gardens or to your poolside patio. 								





















 





standard view rooms 



									enjoy garden views from your juliet balcony or opt for a poolside room on the 2nd or 3rd floor, some with partial sea views. book now for a scenic retreat!
