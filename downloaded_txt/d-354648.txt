for travelers to london, staying in the central london is key to making sure you have the best trip possible.
                    when you are in central london you have easy access to not only all of the major attractions in the city but also to the best entertainment, dining, shopping and business locations. the sidney hotel places you in this perfect spot, close to places like buckingham palace, the london eye, royal albert hall and more. for those traveling on business, the hotel is close by to the pimlico underground station, the london victoria station, victoria tube station and the gatwick express so you can get to whatever office you need to via public transit quickly. you will also be near to the passport offices and the elisabeth conference centre.
