about us
set in a historic house in the heart of jackson hole, the blue lion has been a local's favorite fine dining spot since 1976. the rack of lamb, game options, fish and seasonal local dishes help round out the diverse chef-driven menu. our bar features house crafted cocktails, beer and an extensive wine list. whether you're joining us for an intimate dinner or a get together with friends, the blue lion is a go to for new american cuisine.Â
