about peak
peak scientific is a world leading nitrogen generator manufacturer who supplies & supports ‭laboratory-grade gas generators globally. peak provide highly reliable and robust solutions, validated by the world's leading instrument manufacturers, for many laboratory applications, including lc-ms (liquid chromatography – mass spectrometry) and gc (gas chromatography).our commitment is to provide your laboratory with a reliable gas generation solution that delivers long-term value. we achieve this through exceptional product design and direct global on-site support that is unmatched in our industry.we are on hand to support you and strive to be a valued partner in your success. with service centers and an extensive network of peak-certified field service engineers located across the globe we are on hand to support you directly, delivering exceptional service over the life of our products.

has your gas generator really been approved by your instrument manufacturer?
you might think your gas generator has been approved by your instrument manufacturer when it is in fact merely 'designed for' your particular gc or lc-ms instrument. read on to discover the significant difference.









peak scientific completed strategic office move in north america
laboratory gas generation specialist, peak scientific, relocated to a new office facility in westford, ma, on 1 may 2024. the move came following the company’s recent plans for introducing increased capacity to support growth in its american markets.









peak scientific expands gas generation expertise with acquisition of noblegen
dusty tlp ltd., owner of peak scientific, acquires wirac automation ltd, trading as noblegen. a company specializing in the design, development and manufacture of specialist gas and cryogenic solutions, this is the latest step in the dusty tlp board’s vision to expand its portfolio and develop its range of products.









horizen 24 - save money
for the first time, we have introduced peak-designed compressors which are unique to our gas generators. these compressors reduce power consumption by as much as 55% leading to a reduction in the cost to run, reducing heat output which helps lower air conditioning costs, and reducing the impact on the sensitivity of results from neighboring equipment.









horizen 24 - save space
horizen 24 is the smallest nitrogen gas generator in its class today. but why does that matter to your lab?









horizen 24 - save the planet
as the world, and especially the scientific community as leaders in the movement, is actively working towards a greener, more environmentally-friendly future, our mission was to design and manufacture the most energy-efficient nitrogen gas generator on the market - horizen 24.
