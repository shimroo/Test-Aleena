featured products









 

part of your ride since 1993
lizard skins is an industry leading manufacturer of sporting goods accessories that revolve around the hand-to-grip interface.  the company was founded in mountain biking and has since become recognized throughout the world in road cycling, baseball, fastpitch, golf, hockey, lacrosse, pickleball, power sports and even for industrial applications.  we work with world-renowned athletes at the top of each sport. our products are designed in collaboration with professional athletes, always with an eye toward innovation and change.  our passion for our customers knows no limits as our employees can be found on the road or trail daily putting our company mission to the test!
