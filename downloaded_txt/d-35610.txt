over 65 years of tradition
for decades it has been the goal of sofo's italian market to provide customers with the very best in italian foods, great customer service, and an authentic italian experience. in 2019 the market was remodeled. with a fresh new look, an open-air café and a new layout, sofo's italian market is gearing up to serve an ever-expanding customer base. we are excited to showcase a new line of unique italian items, traditional sofo imports, and locally sourced products from the greater toledo area.
