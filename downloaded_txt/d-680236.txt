our mission
the mission of tempe preparatory academy is to educate students for the lifelong pursuit of truth, goodness, and beauty. tpa graduates have a foundation in the liberal arts of grammar and rhetoric, logic and mathematics, history, natural science, and philosophy. graduates will also have practiced the fine arts of music, poetry, drawing and painting, and drama. tpa graduates are thus prepared for every career, every course of advanced or specialized study, and every kind of leisure.
