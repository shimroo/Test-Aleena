harness the opportunity with oracle partnernetwork 








oracle partnernetwork (opn) streamlines a partner’s journey with oracle. opn members access offerings that help them achieve oracle expertise to gain customer trust and relevancy and grow their business across industries and lines of business. with opn, you choose how to engage with oracle based on your business strategy.

enable your business
track subscriptions
opn tracks are aligned to oracle expertise.  these tracks provide specific resources and tools to help incorporate oracle into your solution, get that solution commercially available to market, attain your expertise and grow your business. which track is right for you?


video: selecting the right track for your business (3:01)








cloud build track
for independent software vendors and other partners that provide commercially available products and/or services built on or integrated with oracle cloud.

learn more






cloud sell track
for partners who resell oracle cloud – since customers are looking for the partner’s value, not solely the ability to resell, consider your value-add when assessing this track selection.

learn more






cloud service track
for partners who implement, deploy, and/or manage oracle cloud.

learn more






industry healthcare track
for partners who provide commercially available products and/or services built with oracle cloud and oracle health technologies.

learn more






license and hardware track
for partners who build, service or sell with oracle software licenses or hardware products.
