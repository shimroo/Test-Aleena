founded in 1978, hess is the premier builder in maryland, washington, dc and virginia, delivering exceptional spaces that inspire. hess manages the planning, design and construction of facilities through the design-bid build, construction management at-risk and construction management-agency delivery methods. hess’ market sector focus includes education, community, municipalities, private office buildings and residential.
