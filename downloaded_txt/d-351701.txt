celebrate girl scouts birthday week!
join us for a week of fun, friendship, and girl scout traditions
  as   we celebrate another year of empowering girls! from night hikes
  and   campfire sing-alongs to sweet treats and s.w.a.p.s. trading,
  there’s   something for everyone to enjoy. whether you’re making ice
  cream,   playing games, or sharing stories around the fire, this is
  the perfect   time to come together and honor the spirit of girl scouts.
don’t miss out on the exciting activities at camp conowingo and
  camp   woodlands! gather your troop, family, and friends to create
  lasting   memories, learn new skills, and celebrate with us. stay
  tuned for   event details and get ready to make this girl scouts
  birthday week the   best one yet!
