urban lofts townhomes is one of the oldest developers and builders in houston. the company was started in 1992 with noted designer larry s. davis’s patented loft-styled townhomes clad in metal for design, strength, and energy conservation. those projects are still in perfect form today and have been the foundation for an ever-increasing level of quality design and construction, but our true success lies in remaining open to and listening to our buyers. urban lofts townhomes also develops in dallas, atlanta, and las vegas.
