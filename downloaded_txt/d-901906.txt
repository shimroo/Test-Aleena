about erickson furniture in everett, wa
erickson furniture is a family-owned discount furniture & mattresses store based in everett, wa. since 1912, erickson furniture has served customers in everett, mukilteo, bothell, lynwood, marysville, snohomish, lake stevens, arlington, mill creek and edmonds with low prices on furniture & mattresses products and top notch customer service.    our knowledge of the furniture & mattresses we sell sets us high above the competition.  erickson furniture is an authorized dealer of such brands as   a america, american leather, archbold furniture, best home furnishings, biltwell, and more.  we can help you select your new furniture & mattresses purchase at any budget.     we look forward to seeing you at erickson furniture, serving everett, mukilteo, bothell, lynwood, marysville, snohomish, lake stevens, arlington, mill creek and edmonds in washington since 1912.
