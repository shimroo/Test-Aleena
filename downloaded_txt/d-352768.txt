where natures majesty meets unparalleled luxury



experience the ultimate retreat at currahee club, featuring a year-round jim fazio-designed golf course, breathtaking lakefront views, and luxurious accommodations. perfect for families seeking to create unforgettable memories, currahee club seamlessly blends rustic charm with modern comfort. discover your haven of relaxation and adventure today.















tee off from the foothills of the blue ridge mountains



immerse yourself in the beauty and challenge of our year-round golf course, meticulously crafted by renowned designer jim fazio. whether you're a seasoned golfer or a beginner, our course offers an unforgettable experience for players of all skill levels.

private events, weddings and golf outings



at currahee club, our elegant clubhouse provides the perfect setting for weddings with stunning views of lake hartwell. our versatile facilities are also ideal for corporate events, offering a productive yet picturesque environment for meetings and retreats. whether it's a wedding, corporate event, or golf outing, currahee club ensures an exceptional experience for all occasions.

dining at the stand alone tavern



our seasonal lunch and dinner menu change to ensure the best quality of ingredients are brought to your table. chef maddie and her team have created dishes that appeal to anything you could be craving. from gourmet burgers in the tavern or snacks at the pool, to the club made desserts that will satisfy any sweet tooth.
