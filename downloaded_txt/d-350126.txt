the rental process 



dream itinspiration can come from anywhere.  whether you saw it on tiktok, at a friends wedding or you just love certain colors we will be able to use those ideas to begin creating your look.  whether you plan to purchase or rent your tuxedo or suit we’ve got you covered!



get fittedto get the process started you simply need to come see us at our store. we do not require appointments, but we’re happy to set one with you to help plan for your event.  you can show us pictures or give us colors you like and we will work with you to build your perfect outfit from there.  once we make a final decision we will take all of your measurements and fit you for the suit of your choosing!  



pick upyou’re almost ready to go.   all that is left for you to do is come in to our store several days before your event to try on your complete tuxedo or suit to ensure perfect sizing before taking it home and heading to the event!
