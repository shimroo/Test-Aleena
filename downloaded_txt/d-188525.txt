about washington squarethere's so much to discover
washington square is oregon’s premier shopping destination, anchored with the state’s largest nordstrom, macy’s, jcpenney, and dicks sporting goods, with a diverse selection of over 170 premier apparel and popular digitally native brands including apple, arc’teryx, aritzia, coach, lego, blue nile, nespresso, pandora, lululemon, the north face, williams sonoma, yeti and many others. once you’ve built up an appetite, the dining options at washington square are sure to please any palate, enjoy delicious restaurants like din tai fung, the cheesecake factory, 85c bakery cafe, nordstrom marketplace cafe, and thirsty lion gastropub & grill.
