    tampa language center

   our institute is committed to making learning the english language a fun and rewarding experience thanks to our dedicated faculty and staff. come and learn english in sunny tampa, florida.
tampa language center has been providing its services to international students, tourists, and residents who want to learn english since 1988.if you have any questions about specific courses and class schedules, please contact us, and we will be happy to give you all of the information that you need.want to keep up with us? follow us on instagram or facebook!

    transfer to universities with no toefl or ielts

students who pass level 6 at tlc will be able to transfer to the university of tampa, hillsborough community college, state college of florida, schiller international university, st. leo university, or full sail university without a toefl or ielts score!!! ask us for more information!
