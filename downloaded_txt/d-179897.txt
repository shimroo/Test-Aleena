optical & electronic
                      research, inc.
                      1967 - 2007

oer  manufactured quality commercial 
                        photographic equipment, including 
                        passport, identification, 
                        and special events cameras mostly based on polaroid instant
                      film. the company was based in reston virgina, 20 miles
                        west of washington dc.
mostly
                      due to the proliferation of inexpensive digital cameras,
                          oer ceased operations in late 2007.  
this
                          site will hopefully give a little history about the
                          company and the products it manufactured.
