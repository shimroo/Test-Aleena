american red cross blood drive 

join us in honoring the life and sacrifice of corporal mark jones with a community blood drive on thursday, february 27th, 2025. help save lives and pay tribute to a hero from the hardeeville police department.				read on...







lowcountry food pantry grocery & hot meal distribution – february 20, 2025 

the lowcountry food pantry will host a free grocery and hot meal distribution on thursday, february 20, 2025, from 5:30 pm to 6:30 pm at hardeeville city hall field. no registration is required. supplies are first-come, first-served.				read on...







groundbreaking ceremony for next phase of juju hutson park project 

the city of hardeeville is excited to break ground on the next phase of the juju hutson park project on feb. 19, 2025, at 11:00 am. this phase will add a pavilion with picnic tables, restrooms, permanent fencing, and expanded lawn space for the community.				read on...
