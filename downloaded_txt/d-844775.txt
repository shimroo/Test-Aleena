outdoor learning and community growth for all















genesee valley is shaping the next generation of leaders, teams, and activists. through nature-based education and outdoor exploration, we empower individuals to discover their strengths, build lasting relationships, and become positive contributors to their communities and the environment.

a place to growsince our start in 1981, genesee valley has made a meaningful impact on over one million participants through our experiential education programming. our dedicated team facilitates an average of 274 programming days each year, offering enriching experiences for all.




    discover who we are
  














wetland restoration at genesee valleyin partnership with the maryland port administration, we're restoring and expanding 7 acres of wetlands to create a thriving ecosystem. this project includes the construction of a state-of-the-art aerial course overlooking our beautiful wetlands, allow us to expand our programming while contributing to a healthier planet. watch our video to learn more!
