for all people to glorify god through knowing christ, becoming like christ, and making christ known.



we are a diverse family of different ages, races and languages. what do we have in common? we love jesus and we are being changed by god's love. with a deep commitment to making steadfast disciples, we are engaging in mission within our communities, and dedicating ourselves to a life of prayer, all empowered by the presence of the holy spirit.

we are a family of disciples who love jesus and are committed to making him known. our mission is to live out the great commission—to make disciples of all nations. this purpose guides everything we do at all nations. we believe church is meant to be lived out in community, not as spectators, but as active followers of jesus, leading others to do the same. are you ready to join us on this journey?
