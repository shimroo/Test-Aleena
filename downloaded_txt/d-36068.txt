pine creek nursery is a breathtakingly beautiful property that truly has it all. as a working nursery, it has an impressive selection of retail and wholesale plants, making it a haven for gardening enthusiasts and professionals alike. but that's not all - this stunning property also doubles as an all season wedding venue, providing a picturesque backdrop for couples to exchange their vows amidst lush greenery and vibrant blooms. and for those who love flowers, pine creek nursery is also home to scattered seeds, a thriving flower farm where rows of colorful blossoms are waiting to be picked and arranged into stunning bouquets, or used as a backdrop for photographers and wedding couples. whether you're a nature lover, a bride to be, or simply someone who appreciates the beauty of the great outdoors, pine creek nursery is a must visit destination.

we are conveniently located off of highway 2 in monroe, nestled in the scenic valley below the cascade mountains. we feel truly blessed to have been serving our community with love and care for over 20 incredible years. we're rooted in to this community, cultivating beauty, connection, and growth. 














our farm cats



pine creek is also home to two sweet farm cats, sulley and bramble, a loving and feisty momma cat/son duo! keep an eye out for them while you’re browsing the nursery and be sure to say hello if you spot them!
