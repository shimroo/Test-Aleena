dls is a swedish privately-owned audio company with high standards. dls was founded in 1979 around the invention of a “state-of-the-art” police radio receiver that could scan all frequencies. from the very beginning, dls chose to focus on exceptional build quality and the highest performance, which since then has been a dls signum.
