welcome to cortland county, where the charm of rural beauty meets the vibrancy of community spirit! nestled in the heart of new york state, cortland county invites you to explore its picturesque landscapes, rich history, and warm hospitality.whether you're here to hike the scenic trails, immerse yourself in the cultural heritage of our historic sites, or simply unwind in the tranquility of our charming towns and villages, cortland county offers something for everyone.
