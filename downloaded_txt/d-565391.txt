engineered abrasives® specializes in the finest automated shot blasting and shot peening equipment designed for your specific application. since 1935, engineered abrasives® has offered a range of custom shot peening and shot blasting machines to meet the requirements of all industries, from automotive to heavy equipment. gears, turbine blades, shafts, connecting rods, engine blocks, and many other parts across various industries are shot blasted and peened with engineered abrasives®-manufactured machines every day. contact us today to find a solution for your shot blasting needs.
