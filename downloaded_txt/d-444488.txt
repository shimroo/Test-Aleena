about international mascot corporation 



we are a custom mascot maker with offices located in edmonton, alberta, canada and atlanta, georgia. we make custom mascots for a variety of industries and are the best place to buy mascot costumes in canada and the united states. we make mascots for sports teams, brands and custom projects. we also provide mascot repair, upgrade and maintenance services.
