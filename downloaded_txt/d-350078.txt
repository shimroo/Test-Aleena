do you love meeting new and interesting people from all over the world? then vzones is the place for you! immerse yourself in the worlds first and longest running virtual world that makes up vzones: dreamscape and newhorizone. these two colorful artistically rendered 2d virtual world known as worldsaway.
when you join vzones, you transform yourself into your digital self and meet others from all over the world. your digital self is turned into a fully customizable avatar. once inside, you will be able to talk, gesture, walk around and explore…play games, build & create homes to live in, win vouchers, buy, sell and lose yourself in the virtual fun.
