foothills marine center

since our founding in 1993, foothills marine has sought to help customers find their dream boat. we've expanded to two stores to better serve the carolinas: morganton, nc, and mooresville, nc. from electrifying bass boats to pontoons fit for your entire family, we've got it all. we have a huge inventory of skeeter®, godfrey®, nitro®, phoenix, tidewater, sportsman®, suntracker, regency®, tahoe®, and hurricane™ deck boats. 


from top to bottom, we strive for excellence. our service department has achieved "mercury premier service" status and "yamaha 5-star certification" for excellence in service and superior customer satisfaction. 


we invite you to browse our extensive inventory! feel free to contact us with any questions you may have!
