city of east lansing prepared for impending winter storm 


city of east lansing officials are closely monitoring the winter weather advisory that is forecasted to begin later this afternoon and continue until midnight resulting in wet, heavy snowfall.						read on...







charter review committee public hearing set for thursday, feb. 20 


the east lansing charter review committee will hold a public hearing at 6:30 p.m. thursday, feb. 20, in room 235 at the east lansing hannah community center, 819 abbot road.						read on...







east lansing public library, police to host ‘paint with the police’ event 


read on...







city of east lansing takes initial step to address lbwl franchise fee ruling 


the city of east lansing has directed the lansing board of water and light (lbwl) to stop collecting franchise fees and refund to customers any fees already collected but not submitted to the city.						read on...







east lansing police chief search begins with community survey 


the city of east lansing is searching for a new police chief for the east lansing police department (elpd) and is asking for feedback from the community via a survey.						read on...







children’s cultural concert series returns 


the east lansing parks, recreation & arts department will host its annual children’s cultural concert series. the events are free and open to all community members.						read on...







nominations now open for 2025 crystal awards 


nominations are now being accepted for the 2025 crystal awards through tuesday, feb. 25.						read on...







city offers free, safe, legal parking for ride-share/food delivery services 


the city of east lansing would like to remind community members of the options it offers for safe and legal free parking for quick drop-offs and pick-ups for ride-share and food delivery services.						read on...







e-filing now available for city of east lansing income taxes 


community members who reside and/or work in the city of east lansing can now file east lansing income taxes online.						read on...







k-12 art exhibit to be installed feb. 11 


community members are invited to visit downtown east lansing for a special exhibit featuring artwork from local students at east lansing public schools and other area educational institutions.						read on...







city offices to close monday, feb. 17, in observance of presidents day 


city of east lansing offices and facilities — except the east lansing hannah community center (elhcc) — will close monday, feb. 17, in observance of presidents day.						read on...







community members urged to stay cautious near snowplows 


with recent snowfall, the city of east lansing would like to remind community members to remain cautious around snowplows.						read on...







city installs new lights at william b. sharp park 


new lights have been installed at william b. sharp park, located at the northeast corner of the abbot road and albert avenue in downtown east lansing.						read on...







2025 winter/spring connect magazine 


community members can browse the connect magazine to learn about programs, classes and activities being offered by the east lansing department of parks, recreation and arts this winter.						read on...







elfd offers tips to stay safe during winter storms 


the east lansing fire department (elfd) has tips for community members to stay safe during winter storms.						read on...







city has published answers to most common questions regarding rental housing exceptions on website 


the city of east lansing’s housing department has recently received a high volume of questions about exceptions to needing a rental license in the city, including the meaning of the term “guest.”						read on...







5g small cell information

round up scholarships remove barriers to participation for community members
