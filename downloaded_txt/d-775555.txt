about us
why choose herb hays furniture & mattress?

			herb hays furniture is a family-owned home furnishing center in hopkinsville, ky. since 1961, herb hays furniture has given shoppers fine name brands, the best selection and service, and better prices on home furnishings, mattresses, and decorator accessories one visit and you'll agree that our knowledge on furniture, mattresses, and home accessories puts us high above the competition by giving you the best furniture shopping experience and continued service. herb hays furniture is an authorized dealer of your favorite brands like ashley, england, la-z-boy, serta, best home furnishings, catnapper, parker house, liberty, hillsdale, coaster, jackson, craftmaster sauder, steinworld, riverside, simmons, and others. you'll find that herb hays furniture can help you select your new mattress or home furnishings at any budget. plus, if you don't find the furniture you are looking for in our showrooms, herb hays furniture has a large selection of special order options at our everyday low prices.
			
			just one stop and you'll agree that we are the regional furniture store of choice. we look forward to helping you with all your furniture and mattress needs! herb hays furniture, serving hopkinsville, fort campbell, oak grove, lafayette, herndon, pembroke, crofton, elkton, trenton, dawson springs, cadiz, guthrie and gracey in kentucky as well as clarksville, sango, dover and bumpus mills, tn, and surrounding areas since 1961.
