about pacific viewthere's so much to discover
aptly named for its sweeping views of the pacific ocean, pacific view features some of the most popular shopping and dining attractions in ventura, ca. shoppers can visit their favorite stores including target, macy's, jcpenney, sephora, 24 hour fitness, ross, victoria’s secret, and grab a bite to eat at delicious restaurants like wood ranch, red robin, and california pizza kitchen.
