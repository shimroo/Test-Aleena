about us 



rysheron irish wolfhounds is dedicated to breeding only exceptional animals that demonstrate the requisite type, temperament and soundness that constitutes this majestic breed. 



we have lived with this noble breed since 1976 and fallen head over heels in love. we are very selective in our breeding practices, producing just one to three very special litters each year. our kennel is small and conscientious, and we plan to keep it that way. our puppies are raised with plenty of love and attention, and we are very proud of their wonderful temperaments.rysheron irish wolfhounds has been breeding, raising and showing exceptional irish wolfhounds for many years. we are proud members and in good standing with many all breed and breed clubs.
