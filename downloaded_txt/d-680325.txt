charles started his career in front of the camera, spending over six years on the nbc daytime drama passions. soon after, he made the move to primetime. spending a season on the wb sitcom half and half along with numerous guest-starring roles on popular tv shows such as csi, melrose place, and my boys. his passion for storytelling later led him to journalism and a career of covering impactful stories.
