welcome to
spicer's boat city
spicer's boat city, located in houghton lake, michigan is your one-stop shop for the new or pre-owned boat or vehicle you're looking for! we carry a large assortment of accessories, apparel, parts, and more! we also provide in-house financing, service and maintenance.
we carry new and pre-owned boats from harris pontoon, four winns, bayliner, lund pontoons, scarab jet boats, bennington, sylvan & starcraft deckboats, and mercury and mercruiser.
spicer's boat city also carries a large variety of new and used powersport vehicles, such as, utility-vehicles & side by sides. can-am atvs, commander, defender, and maverick utv's, polaris atvs, ranger & rzr. polaris snowmobiles. ski-doo snowmobiles. sea-doo, yamaha wave runner, and spark personal watercraft.
spicer's boat city is conveniently located near the areas of houghton lake, higgins lake, st. helen, grayling, west branch, gladwin, gaylord, detroit, cadillac, lake city, clare, harrison, lansing, traverse city, kalkaska, oscoda, tawas, alpena, flint, bay city, midland, saginaw, mt. pleasant, and surrounding towns and northern michigan.
