welcome to blackstone steakhouse
long island’s premiere steakhouse, blackstone is an architectural masterpiece that offers an unforgettable dining experience. dine or enjoy cocktails al fresco in our outdoor lounge featuring ambient lighting, lush landscaping and ultra-comfortable oversize booths and seating.reservationswe are proud to be certified by the official kobe beef association.
