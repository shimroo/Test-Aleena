in this section






memorial hospital miramar is located in miramar, fl and offers a range of healthcare services in a patient- and family-centered care environment.
emergency care
our adult and children's emergency departments provide convenient, family-focused, 24-hour emergency room care for minor illnesses, injuries and chronic medical conditions. we're specially staffed and equipped to assist the entire family, with separate waiting rooms and treatment areas for adults and children.
 
for pediatric minor emergencies, you can schedule your appointment online. appointments are available seven days a week for pediatric patients.
