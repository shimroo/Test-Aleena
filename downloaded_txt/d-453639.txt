systecon
who we are
systecon, an equans company, is a leading manufacturer and innovator of modular utility systems. we began building pumping systems in 1971 and have since expanded into custom modular utility solutions, including central plants, central plant controls, and our patented variprime® and critichill® systems.  
for over 50 years, we’ve worked with our customers – including owners, gcs, and consulting engineers to provide them with the best solution for their projects. systecon’s factory-built systems offer less risk, shorter project schedules, and improved quality and safety compared to traditional field-built systems. we have markets across the u.s. and our work spans the globe with successful projects on five continents.
