who we are
family owned and operated, island delights™ believes in the goodness of all things that grow. located in seville, ohio, we create, package and distribute a variety of products from the earth under our 2 brands: island delights candies™ and grove harvest®.
we believe in tradition. at island delights candies, we make nostalgic confections the same way as generations before us, taking you back to simpler times. we believe in wholesome goodness. grove harvest snacks are deliciously indulgent, crafted using fresh ingredients harvested from the tropics. you’ll taste the wonderfully better-for-you difference in our irresistible snack blends.
