gsr research officer scheme
  3 february 2025 — guidance


join us as a research officer to launch a brilliant career in government social research.

 







government social research degree apprenticeship programme
  4 february 2025 — guidance


the gsrdap is a four-year apprenticeship programme which offers a first of its kind opportunity to gain real working experience while studying for an undergraduate degree.

 







how to apply for a government social research sandwich student placement
 18 october 2024 — guidance


the government economic and social research (gesr) team run a 12-month sandwich student placement scheme aimed at students who are seriously considering a career in the government social research (gsr) profession after graduation.

 22 may 2023 — corporate report
