supporting australian designers for over 30 yearsfounded in sydney in 1990, south pacific fabrics is australia’s foremost supplier of luxury textiles, lighting, furniture, and passementerie. with flagship showrooms in sydney, melbourne, and brisbane, and agency partnerships nationwide, we are proud to bring the world’s most sought-after interior products to the australian design community. we share a commitment to excellence with both the designers we serve and the brands we represent.
