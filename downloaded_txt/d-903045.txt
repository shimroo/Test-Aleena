 learn about kingston pizza
  open since 1984, our pizza restaurant in kingston is a household name in southern rhode island with an impressive ten locations offering delivery. kingston pizza has won a variety of best pizza and best specialty pizza awards, and we appreciate all the positivity and support of our staff, as well as our customers.
