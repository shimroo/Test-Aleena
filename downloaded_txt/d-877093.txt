voestalpine high performance metals (australia) pty ltd



the high performance metals division of the voestalpine group focuses on the production and processing of high-performance materials and customer-specific services. the division is the global market leader in tool steel and one of the leading suppliers of other products made of high-performance materials. the most important customer segments are the automotive, oil and gas exploration, mechanical engineering, consumer goods and aerospace industries.
we support our customer in the following product segments:

high performance metals (tool steels, high speed steels)
engineering steels
stainless alloys
non ferrous
special materials
precision strip
special forgings
profiles
engineered products
additive manufacturing (powder & parts)



the voestalpine high performance metals division offers its products in more than 100 countries and has its own trade organizations more than 50 of them. the major markets are europe, asia and america.
































































































 


voestalpine high performance metals
voestalpine high performance metals (australia) pty ltd is the sales company in australia of the high performance metals division of the voestalpine group. the division focuses on technologically demanding product segments and is the worldwide market leader for tool steel and other speciality steels.
