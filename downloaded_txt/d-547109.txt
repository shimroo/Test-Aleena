customer commitment
at harper infiniti, we know you expect the best, so we strive to give you a personalized shopping experience. for our commitment to customers, we earned the infiniti award of excellence 15 times. in addition, our dealership is a two-time winner of the knoxville news sentinel top workplaces award.
to help expedite the loan process, you can fill out a finance application online and use our calculator to determine your monthly payment amount. on all new and most pre-owned vehicles, we offer harper care, which is an unlimited time and miles powertrain warranty. this feature also provides you with free loaner vehicles with scheduled service and complimentary car wash with every service visit.
our highly qualified technicians, who are automotive service excellence (ase) certified, aim to give you exceptional service in a timely manner, while our parts department can help with genuine infiniti replacement parts and accessories. you can easily schedule service appointments online or call our department directly. our service department remains open until 6 p.m. on weeknights.


massive inventory
found in knoxville, tennessee, harper infiniti gives customers a car-buying service like no other. founder tom harper opened our doors in 1991, and since then customers have visited us for our award-winning customer service, excellent prices, and dedication to the community. come by and see what we have to offer.
customers come from across the knoxville area to take advantage of our huge inventory of new, used, and certified pre-owned vehicles. they love several of our luxury suv infiniti models, such as the midsize qx60, which includes intuitive technology and a spacious cabin. two other favorites are the compact qx50, which gets excellent gas mileage and features plenty of cargo space, and the large qx80, which seats seven and has numerous standard safety features.
if you're interested in smaller luxury vehicles, consider the infiniti q50 sedan or q60 coupe. the q50 offers agile handling and an optional powerful v-6 engine, while the q60 has a high-end interior with standard leatherette upholstery.


charitable giving
at harper infiniti, we seek to give back to the community that has supported us for so many years. we consistently support several local organizations, including the knoxville inner city kids outreach inc. (kicko), dogwood arts festival, mcclung museum of natural history and culture, junior achievement of east tennessee, and emerald youth foundation. we also help national affiliations, including the boys & girls club, united way, american heart association, and american cancer society.
we also established our own foundation, ruthie's fund, which is run by our founder. this dollar-for-dollar charity donates all its funds to the families of cancer patients. in addition, we sponsor multiple track days, audio show trips, and cars and coffee events.
when you come to harper infiniti, you will be amazed at our huge inventory of luxury vehicles available at affordable prices. there's a reason why we're one of the best dealerships in eastern tennessee. experience firsthand how our commitment to customers and the community stands out above the rest.
