clinical laboratory products & solutions  

working with you to power the moments that matter. our clinical diagnostic products and services are used in hospitals, reference laboratories and physician office settings around the world to deliver clinical and operational insights. regardless of the size of your laboratory, our diagnostic products and solutions can help you deliver greater insights and accelerate care.




explore clinical diagnostic solutions

elevate your laboratory operations and deliver trusted test results with advanced clinical laboratory solutions. our in vitro diagnostic solutions include innovative assays, novel biomarkers, industry-leading analytical technology, and attentive professional services tailored to the needs of laboratories of every size.
