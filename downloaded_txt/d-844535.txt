the schwag
 the schwag is a band of musicians dedicated to carrying on the vibe and music of the legendary
			grateful dead. founded in 1991 the schwag has performed over 3000 concerts all over the united states,
			sharing stages with legends such as george clinton & parliament funkadelic, keller williams, big brother
			& the holding company, and new riders of the purple sage. the schwag band is now stronger than ever
			on a perpetual tour performing a different show every night of high energy organic interpretations of the
			grateful dead.
