    retained leadership searches
  














strategenius is the premier search firm for schools seeking excellence in candidates for teaching and leadership positions. we partner with schools committed to equity, connect them to our diverse network, and match them with exceptional talent. strategenius is one of the only bipoc-owned search firms dedicated to serving independent schools.



how we support member schools and candidates



partnershipdevelop deep relationships with member schools to increase their capacity to become more welcoming, inclusive, and supportive of all forms of diversity.leadershipmodel and spread best practices on attracting, recruiting, developing, and supporting people from all identities in independent schools, creating inclusive and equitable environments.



mentorship provide coaching, mentorship and opportunities for all groups, including underrepresented groups (people of color, women, lgbtq+, neurodiverse, etc.), to succeed and take leadership in independent schools.advocacy  advocate for equity and diversity among candidates within our schools.

how we work with you



member schoolsstrategenius partners with schools committed to advancing diversity, equity, and inclusion, supporting efforts to increase representation within faculty, staff, and leadership and fostering a truly inclusive environment. while all schools can benefit from strategenius' expertise, our member schools gain unique advantages through the strategenius member schools program.membership signifies a school’s deep dedication to diverse recruitment and retention practices. member schools receive priority access to our wide network of educators, along with exclusive benefits such as on-campus visits, unlimited job postings on our website, and promotion through our network. additionally, members enjoy discounted consulting rates for services including bias mitigation, diversity audits, leadership training, and more. membership also provides access to special programs and opportunities like our member school spotlights, the inspired workshop series, and the virtual career fair and networking event.



candidateswhen you work with strategenius, you receive free personalized, expert advice on making sure you put your very best foot forward beginning with your materials, during your interviews, through the hiring process, and as you embark on your new position. even if you are just getting started on your teaching career or are new to working with independent schools, we can help you navigate the transition and find the perfect match. we have helped hundreds of people throughout their career, many who have started as interns and have moved through institutions with increasing levels of responsibility. we want to grow with you! let us help you explore your options and illuminate a path that identifies and matches your talents, passion, and experience with a school.
