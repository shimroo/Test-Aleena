best wedding venue


















































our venue



long island gem, the meadow club of port jefferson has been serving your catering needs for generations with exceptional food quality and service. with three newly renovated unique ballrooms available, the meadow club ensures all of your needs and wishes are carried out with impeccable detail and expertise. our in-house event planning services will make the process effortless from beginning to end. partnered with curry club @ saghar in port jefferson, we offer a diverse menu including classic italian, to exotic indian.












































































weddings



the meadow club serves as the perfect venue to host your most monumental day - your wedding. with tranquil grounds that create the perfect backdrop for outdoor ceremonies to the extravagant interior that welcomes guests with a grand staircase and chandelier, you'll experience a day of beauty and serenity at the meadow club.




























    plan your wedding
  
























social















our banquet hall services the entire long island community for an array of events including sweet 16's, birthdays, anniversaries, christenings, communions, bar/bat mitzvahs, and any special day that requires a celebration! please call or stop by for a consultation appointment so that we can discuss your next special event! 
















    plan your social event
  




























corporate



let our team assist you with an unforgettable corporate event for you and your guests. our professional team can handle all of your event venue and catering needs. with the meadow club, you get every convenience to ensure your special event is one to remember along with a stunning venue.
