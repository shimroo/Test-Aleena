weather alert





extreme cold warning from tue 12:00 am cst until tue 10:00 am cst














weather alert


...extreme cold warning remains in effect from midnight tonight to
10 am cst tuesday...

* what...dangerously cold temperatures of 10 to 30 degrees below
zero and wind chills as low as 45 below zero expected over
northwest wisconsin.

* where...price, ashland, bayfield, burnett, douglas, iron, sawyer,
and washburn counties. this includes the tribal lands of the red
cliff band, the northwestern area of the lac du flambeau band, the
st. croix band in burnett county, the lac courte oreilles
reservation and the bad river reservation. other locations
including the apostle islands national lakeshore and madeline
island.

* when...from midnight tonight to 10 am cst tuesday.

* impacts...the dangerously cold wind chills as low as 45 below zero
could cause frostbite on exposed skin in as little as 5 to 10
minutes. frostbite and hypothermia will occur if unprotected skin
is exposed to these temperatures.

precautionary/preparedness actions...

if you must go outside, dress in layers including a hat, face mask,
and gloves.
