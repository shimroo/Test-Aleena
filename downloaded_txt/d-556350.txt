40 years of reliability: powering global industries with automatic self-cleaning water filtration



optimize your operations with tekleen's highly water-efficient, automatic self-cleaning water filters—engineered to reduce water waste and support best-in-class sustainable practices.















why is tekleen the #1 choice in self cleaning water filters?




cost effective: automates the cleaning cycle to save you maintenance time and operating costs, and improve your bottom line.outstanding service: 35 years of industry leading expertise, short delivery times and outstanding local service you can count on 24/7.



high quality performance: we continuously innovate to deliver high performance filters with filtration ranges from 1,000 microns to 2 micron. efficient and reliable: our filters meet the highest nsf/ansi standard 61 for drinking water system components, and are designed to asme boiler and pressure vessel code and section viii standards. sustainable: our highly efficient automatic filters help you reduce water waste and adapt to best-in-class green practices seamlessly


























how do our filters work?



tekleen automatic self-cleaning filters use stainless steel screens and an automated backwash system to remove debris in under 10 seconds without interrupting water flow. this ensures consistent, high-efficiency filtration down to 2 microns, eliminating manual cleaning and downtime.
