about the lomega district 



lomega public schools is a special place where academic excellence meets athletic prowess, and community involvement is at the heart of everything we do. for decades, our dedicated teachers and staff have been committed to providing generations of students with a quality education that stands the test of time.lomega’s athletes have proven time and time again that determination and talent go hand in hand. academics and athletics are just part of the equation though. lomega has also cultivated a successful ffa chapter with multiple state and american ffa degrees. from our lineup of extracurricular activities to clubs that spark passion, students are encouraged to explore their interests and unleash their potential.lomega public schools is not just a place to learn – it’s a community that instills values, shapes character, and prepares students to lead purposeful lives.
