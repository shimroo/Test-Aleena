important notice






find out more on how we are responding to the coronavirus (covid-19) situation by clicking here. 







holiday closing 







closed on monday, february 17th in observance of  the president’s day holiday. 











fraud alert 







we are seeing a number of text message and phone call scams for members and non-members, and we want you to be aware so you can be prepared! these text messages and phone calls are not from aodfcu or anyone affiliated with the credit union. we will never ask for your debit card pin or cvv nor your online banking information. for more info., visit here. 







all other aodfcu branch lobbies and drive thrus are open to serve members.  







mask update 







*mask are preferred when in lobbies / teller line. **when visiting member service offices you will be asked to wear a mask.  







the safety of our members, employees, and community is always our top priority. please continue to check back here for the most up-to-date information from aodfcu.
