sunward cohousingann arbor, mi
we are an intentional community located on 20 acres of woods and ponds one-half mile west of ann arbor, michigan. are you ready to live, work, and play in a community?














from planet community: cohousing communities are collaborative neighborhoods created with intention and a little ingenuity. this video gives an introduction to the 3 ann arbor cohousing communities: sunward, great oak, and touchstone. 

what is cohousing?a place were everyone knows your name.a pedestrian community with a car-free campus (vehicles are parked on the periphery).sustainable living.common facilities. good connections with neighbors.self-governing. community members are active participants in governing and maintaining buildings and grounds.innovative answer to today’s environmental and social problems.for more information about cohousing in general, visit cohousing.org
























sunward cohousing is…90 residents including all ages. a mix of families, singles, and retirees.40 private homes clustered on 5 acres. 20 acres, including woods, prairie, and pond.diverse cultural backgrounds, ethnicities, and spiritual & secular paths.  legally a condo association with private homes.built in 1998; almost half of the founding families still live here!bound together by our commitment to live together in community.learn more about sunward cohousing on our about page
