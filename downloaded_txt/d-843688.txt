buffalo chophouse
buffalo’s best prime aged steaks & seafood

buffalo chophouse is western new york’s premier steakhouse, serving only the finest usda prime steaks as well as a selection of fresh, succulent seafood. located in the heart of buffalo’s theater district, buffalo chophouse is the perfect place to begin or end your evening, celebrate a special occasion, or simply treat yourself to the most fabulous dining experience offered in the wny area. whether you prefer filet mignon, strip steaks, ribeye or lobster, our knowledgeable service staff and reputable kitchen team offer our guests an exclusive dining experience. make a selection from our expansive wine cellar, sip on handcrafted cocktails prepared by our veteran bartenders, and be sure to indulge with a decadent dessert to conclude your memorable experience.









established in 2002, buffalo chophouse remains wny’s most dependable steakhouse for the ultimate dining experience.
