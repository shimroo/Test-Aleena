welcome to w. n. morehouse truck line, inc.

join our family-owned trucking business, proudly located in omaha, nebraska. with 90 years of service, we specialize in refrigerated product haulage throughout the midwest united states.
established in 1932 by william newel morehouse, we've evolved into a trusted name in the industry. offering rewarding trucking jobs and exciting career opportunities, we're managed by the fourth generation and employ over 130 dedicated professionals.
with an average fleet of 100 trucks and 170 trailers, we ensure reliable transportation solutions for our clients. we prioritize our employees, striving to create a clean, friendly, and comfortable work environment.
explore the possibilities of a fulfilling trucking career with us. contact us today to learn more about available trucking jobs and start your journey with our supportive team.
